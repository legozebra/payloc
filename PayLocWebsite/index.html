Meet our Geofencing, Automated Clock In/Out App

For years, businesses have been using low-tech, physcial punch cards clock in and out of their work. These cards can take hours to sort and input into spreadsheets, wasting managers' time.

Enter our solution: The LocIn App provides a simple way for employees to automatically clock in and out without having to deal with punch cards or memorize a certain number. The app is geofenced, which means you can only clock in when you are physically on location.
