Short Overview.

PayLoc aims to make traditional punchcards obsolete by allowing employees to be able to clock in and out of their shifts using only their smartphone via the PayLoc Mobile App. The app utilizes geofencing technology to ensure that each request made in the app is both authentic and secure.

And it all integrates directly with PayLoc’s payroll tools to generate recommendations on how much a employee should be paid depending on how long they’ve worked.

  • Interactive Calendar View
  • Geofencing Clock In and Out App
  • Easy Smart Shift Change Request
  • Auto-populate Thousands of Shifts
  • Payroll Generation from Shifts
  • Pay Individually or with a spreadsheet
  • Direct Deposit

Scheduling Platform

PayLoc’s scheduling platform is great for employees to be able to request shift changes with one another. Employees can simply select a shift that they want to switch with on the Calendar view, and the request will be sent to both the other employee involved in the shift change as well as the manager for approval.

We aim to shorten the shift changing process down to the amount of time it would take managers to receive and respond to a text message.
